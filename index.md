# Privacy Policy — FINNER

**Effective date:** 2026-07-04
**Last updated:** 2026-07-04

FINNER ("the App") is a personal financial planner application. This Privacy
Policy explains what information the App handles, how it is stored, and the
choices available to you.

> 🇮🇩 **Versi Bahasa Indonesia tersedia di bagian bawah halaman ini.**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

The App does **not** collect personal information about you and does **not**
transmit your data to our servers — we do not operate any backend server.
The App works fully offline.

The App stores the following information **locally on your device**:

- **Financial data you input:** transactions, accounts (bank/e-wallet/credit
  card names and balances), categories, budgets, savings targets, and debts
  or receivables you record.
- **Application settings:** your display name (optional, used only for the
  dashboard greeting), language, theme color, and dark mode preference.
- **In-app purchase state:** a flag indicating whether you have purchased
  the Pro upgrade via Google Play.

All of the above is stored in a local SQLite database within the App's
private storage directory on your device. We have no access to it.

## 2. Third-Party Services

### 2.1 Google Play Billing
When you purchase the Pro upgrade, Google Play handles payment processing.
We never see your credit card or payment details. Google Play returns only a
confirmation that the purchase was completed. See
[Google Play Terms of Service](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/).

### 2.2 Google Fonts
On first launch with an internet connection, the App may download font files
from Google Fonts servers for display purposes. This request does not include
any of your financial data. See
[Google Fonts Privacy](https://developers.google.com/fonts/faq/privacy).

The App contains **no advertising and no analytics/tracking SDKs**.

## 3. Backups

The App provides a manual "Backup Data" feature that exports your data to a
JSON file. **You choose** where this file is sent (e.g., your own Google
Drive) via the Android share menu. The backup file contains your financial
data — store it somewhere you trust. We never receive a copy. Restoring a
backup replaces the data on your device after your confirmation.

## 4. Data Deletion

Your data never leaves your device unless you export it yourself. To delete
all data, use **Settings → Reset All Data** inside the App, or simply
uninstall the App. Backup files you created are under your own control and
should be deleted separately if desired.

## 5. Permissions

The App requests only the INTERNET permission, used exclusively for Google
Play Billing and one-time font download. It does not access your contacts,
location, camera, SMS, or files outside what you explicitly pick during
backup/restore.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is a general-audience financial tool and is not directed at children
under 13. It does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including
children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this
page with an updated "Last updated" date.

## 8. Contact

Questions about this policy: **reinhart.juniars@gmail.com**

# Kebijakan Privasi — FINNER (Bahasa Indonesia)

**Tanggal berlaku:** 04-07-2026 · **Terakhir diperbarui:** 04-07-2026

## 1. Informasi yang Kami Kumpulkan

FINNER **tidak mengumpulkan** informasi pribadi Anda dan **tidak mengirim**
data Anda ke server kami — kami tidak mengoperasikan server apa pun.
Aplikasi bekerja sepenuhnya offline.

Data berikut disimpan **hanya di perangkat Anda**: transaksi keuangan, akun
(bank/e-wallet/kartu kredit), kategori, budget, target tabungan,
utang-piutang, serta pengaturan aplikasi (nama panggilan, bahasa, tema).
Status pembelian Pro disimpan sebagai penanda lokal. Semuanya berada di
database SQLite di penyimpanan privat aplikasi — kami tidak punya akses.

## 2. Layanan Pihak Ketiga

- **Google Play Billing** — memproses pembayaran upgrade Pro. Kami tidak
  pernah melihat detail pembayaran Anda.
- **Google Fonts** — unduhan file font satu kali saat aplikasi pertama
  dibuka dengan koneksi internet. Tidak menyertakan data keuangan Anda.

Aplikasi **tidak memuat iklan dan tidak memakai SDK analitik/pelacak**.

## 3. Backup

Fitur "Backup Data" mengekspor data Anda ke file JSON, dan **Anda sendiri
yang memilih** tujuannya (mis. Google Drive pribadi) lewat menu share
Android. Kami tidak pernah menerima salinannya. Pulihkan backup akan
mengganti data di perangkat setelah konfirmasi Anda.

## 4. Penghapusan Data

Gunakan **Setting → Reset Semua Data**, atau uninstall aplikasi. File backup
yang pernah Anda buat berada dalam kendali Anda sendiri.

## 5. Izin Aplikasi

Hanya izin INTERNET (untuk Play Billing dan unduhan font). Tidak mengakses
kontak, lokasi, kamera, SMS, maupun file selain yang Anda pilih sendiri saat
backup/restore.
